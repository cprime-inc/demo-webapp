Summer Getaway Special: Saturn Summer Sizzle

Ever dreamt of seeing Saturn? Now, you and your team can experience all the magic of our sixth and sexiest planet.

For a limited time only, Teams in Space is offering discounted group rates to teams of eight or more. Groups will receive a 20% discount as part of our Summer Saturn Sizzle.

Take advantage of this once-in-a-century offer and check out all the features that ‘Planet with the rings’ has to offer. Book a five day stay at either the famous Red Titan Hotel or the newly opened Saturn’s Rings Resort.

Overwhelmed by the large-crowds on Jupiter? See what our solar system’s second biggest planet has to offer. Avoid the typical tourist route and enjoy guided moon tours from our local partner Phobos and Deimos Tours. Skate the ice rings or surf the asteroid belt. Book your flights and hotel at the same time to receive a 20% discount. You will undoubtably leave with a new perspective on Earth – literally!
